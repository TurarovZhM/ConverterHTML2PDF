VP0_VelocityCorrection

(chamberwise) switch to compute free surfaces (UCV)

VP0_VelocityCorrection = 1
Default: VP0_VelocityCorrection = 0
By default, this correction (Chorin-correction) is switched off for the "vp-"-option, as we assume the velocity to be sufficiently close to its appropriate value of div(v). However, theoretically it is not wrong to perform the correction, see equation (24) in Meshfree_Methods_Proceeding_Paper_Jefferies_Kuhnert_17042014.pdf or equation (2.6) in DOCUMATH_ScalingOfLinearSystem_MxV.pdf. There is one risk: if the correction pressure (%ind_c%) is corrupt, that will then also mess up the velocity.
