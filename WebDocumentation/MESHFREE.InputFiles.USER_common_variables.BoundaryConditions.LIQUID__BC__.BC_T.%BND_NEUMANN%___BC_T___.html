%BND_NEUMANN%

temperature Neumann boundary condition

Neumann (second-type) boundary condition for temperature.
Syntax:
BC_T($xyz$) = (%BND_NEUMANN%, )
Example 1: Constant temperature gradient
BC_T($wall$) = (%BND_NEUMANN%, 10)
Applies a constant temperature gradient of at the boundary with BC-flag $wall$. Example 2: Constant heat flux
begin_alias{} "heatflux" = "100" # W/m^2 "heatConductivity" = "2" # W/(mK) end_alias ... BC_T($wall$) = (%BND_NEUMANN%, [&heatflux&/&heatConductivity&])
