%ind_ETA_sm%

total viscosity, consisting of physical, turbulent, and additional numerical viscosities; unit: Ns/(m^2)

The variable represents the total viscosity used in the VelocityAlgorithm .
%ind_ETA_sm% is smoothed before usage in VelocityAlgorithm in order to stabilize the numerical solution by relaxing jumps, especially towards the boundary. For smoothing, see also COMP_nbSmooth_Eta and COMP_facSmooth_Eta. For stability, see also COMP_AdjustEtaEff. %ind_ETA_sm% is the smoothed %ind_ETA_eff% if
For turbulence modelling, see KepsilonAlgorithm. The paramter can be defined by COEFF_mue.
