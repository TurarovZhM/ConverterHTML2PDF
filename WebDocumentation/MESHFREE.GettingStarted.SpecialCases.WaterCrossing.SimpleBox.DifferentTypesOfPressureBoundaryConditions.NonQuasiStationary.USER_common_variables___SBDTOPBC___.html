USER_common_variables

simple box driving through a channel of water: USER_common_variables.dat

See especially the first lines where we establish several instances of the box-in-channel example by include_Ucv{} .
include_Ucv{Ucv_SinglePoolWithBox.dat} parameters{_CH_=1, _BC_=BND_none} # load the box-in-channel geometry into chamber 1 and apply %BND_none% boundary conditions for %ind_p_dyn% include_Ucv{Ucv_SinglePoolWithBox.dat} parameters{_CH_=2, _BC_=BND_wall} # load the box-in-channel geometry into chamber 2 and apply %BND_wall% boundary conditions for %ind_p_dyn% include_Ucv{Ucv_SinglePoolWithBox.dat} parameters{_CH_=3, _BC_=BND_slip} # load the box-in-channel geometry into chamber 3 and apply %BND_slip% boundary conditions for %ind_p_dyn% include_Ucv{Ucv_SinglePoolWithBox.dat} parameters{_CH_=4, _BC_=BND_NEUMANN} # load the box-in-channel geometry into chamber 2 and apply %BND_NEUMANN% boundary conditions for %ind_p_dyn% include_Ucv{Ucv_SinglePoolWithBox.dat} parameters{_CH_=5, _BC_=BND_AVERAGE} # load the box-in-channel geometry into chamber 2 and apply %BND_AVERAGE% boundary conditions for %ind_p_dyn%
