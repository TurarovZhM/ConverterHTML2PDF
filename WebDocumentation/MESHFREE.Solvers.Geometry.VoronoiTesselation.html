VoronoiTesselation

how to tesselate a given cloud of points

Given a set of points around the origin. In order to produce a complete Voronoi cell, one has to execute the algorithm of SingleFace for ever point in the point cloud . Many of the points will produce void faces due to extinctng intersections. However, some of the points will produce regular faces, which will form a closed shell around the origin. The volume of the cell is computed
