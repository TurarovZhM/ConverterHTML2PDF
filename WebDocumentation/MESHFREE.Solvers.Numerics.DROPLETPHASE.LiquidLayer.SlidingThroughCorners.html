SlidingThroughCorners

DROPLETPHASE - Modeling of liquid layers - modelling the slip of points through corners

In sharp corner/bends of the geometry, a water film gets stuck, unless the thickness of the film reaches a certain level. In the numerical modelling, the points are always at the surface of the rigid geometry, so actually, points will always get stuck in certain situations, regardless of the modelled height of the water film. In order to prevent this, we allow, that points keep a partition of their kinetic energy (or all), if they go through corners.
