ImplicitEuler

This algorithm solves the semi-discrete ODE-system
with the implicit Euler method which is of order 1:
The ODE-system comes from the spatial discretization of velocity and pressure in solve_V_2. Both vp- and v-- can be solved. The time integration scheme can be controlled by time_integration_impl and resp. for the velocity by time_integration_impl_solve_v.
