Ucv_SinglePoolWithBox

simple box driving through a channel of water: Ucv_SinglePoolWithBox.dat

Have a special look at the parameters _CH_ (chamber index) and _BC_ (requested pressure boundary condition). These parameters are replaced with their given values in USER_common_variables upon read in .
