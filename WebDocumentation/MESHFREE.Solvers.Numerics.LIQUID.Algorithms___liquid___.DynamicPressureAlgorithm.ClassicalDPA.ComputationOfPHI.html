Numerically, we have several choices to compute this term, they all might differe depending on the approximatio nquality of the differential operators.
  • Variant 1: The formal way from the equation above yields
  • Variant 2: We try to isolate the divergence of velocity by
  • Variant 3: take the divergence of the stationary part of the substantial velocity
  • Variant 4: take the divergence of the true substantial derivative of the velocity (use as computed due to FLIQUID_ConsistentPressure_Version )
Especially variants 2 and 3 explicitly contain terms with the divergence of velocity . Even for incompressible flows, the numerical evaluation of this term will not entirely be zero. In order to neglect the divergence anyways, use the option FLIQUID_ConsistentPressure_UseDivV . The hope is to gain smoothness of the pressure solutiuon.
